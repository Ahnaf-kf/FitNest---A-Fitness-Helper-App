# FitNest

**Discipline, Tracked. Progress, Earned.**

No guessing. No excuses. FitNest is a fitness tracking application built for those who demand precision. Monitor every workout, every rep, every meal, and every night's rest with military-grade accuracy.

## Features

### Workouts
Stop guessing. Start tracking. Log every exercise, set, and rep with military precision. Monitor your progress over time, enforce consistent gains, and visualize the payoff with detailed progress charts that reward your discipline.

### Cardio
Log every session to maximize your cardiovascular engine. FitNest provides the data required to balance strict aerobic training with strength, ensuring no effort is wasted on average results. Build your foundation. Document your edge.

### Meals
Rule your kitchen. Implement custom meal plans and track macros precisely. Whether your target is composition change or pure performance fuel, FitNest provides the metrics you need to ensure you are building, not just guessing.

### Sleep
Discipline demands recovery. Prioritize restoration with precise sleep tracking. Analyze duration and quality to correlate exactly how rest impacts your output. Optimize recovery—because gains aren't made in the gym; they are made in bed.

## Tech Stack

- MongoDB | Express.js | React | Node.js (MERN Stack)
- HTML, CSS
- Deployed on Vercel
